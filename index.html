// ABOUT ENGLAND

A Nation Woven From Centuries of Story

England is a country that has shaped the modern world in more ways than any other. Its literature, music, science, and law have left marks on every continent. From the Magna Carta to the Industrial Revolution, from Shakespeare to The Rolling Stones — England's cultural output is simply unmatched.

Yet beneath the global icons lies a nation of incredible local diversity: ancient dialects, regional festivals, stone-walled villages, and pub traditions that date back centuries.
